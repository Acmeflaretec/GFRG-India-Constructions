What is GFRG Construction?

GFRG (Glass Fiber Reinforced Gypsum) construction, also known as Rapid wall Panel, is a modern, energy-efficient building technology. Originating from Australia in the 1990s and introduced to India in the 2010s, GFRG panels are used extensively due to their superior strength, durability, and environmental benefits. These panels are suitable for load-bearing and non-load-bearing walls, as well as intermediary floor and roof slabs. GFRG technology offers high resistance to fire, heat, water, termites, rot, and corrosion, making buildings resilient to earthquakes, cyclones, and fire.
